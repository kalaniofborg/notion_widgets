# Notion Widgets & Templates

A curated collection of **custom Notion widgets, templates, and UI utilities** built to enhance productivity, organization, and system design,  especially for **business owners, creatives, and tech-focused workflows**.

This repository is designed to be:
-  Minimal & aesthetic
-  System-driven
-  Modular and easy to reuse
-  Developer-friendly

---

## What's Included

This repo may include (and will continue to grow):

-  **Custom Notion Widgets**
  - Progress trackers
  - Goal & habit widgets
  - Countdown timers
  - Status indicators
  - API-powered widgets (where applicable)

-  **Notion Templates**
  - Master dashboards
  - Business & brand hubs
  - Content planning systems
  - Goal trackers
  - Financial & strategy planners
  - Tech / cybersecurity study trackers

-  **UI Components**
  - Styled embeds
  - Minimal aesthetic widgets
  - Dark / luxury-themed layouts
  - Custom icons & visual elements

---

## Purpose

This project exists to:
- Centralize reusable Notion components
- Make advanced Notion systems more accessible
- Blend **design, productivity, and technical thinking**
- Provide templates that scale with real workflows

Whether you're building a personal system or a full business OS, these components are designed to plug in seamlessly.
